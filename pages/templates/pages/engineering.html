Portfolio Engineering

Balance is about being prepared, rather than betting on the future

Our goal is to construct portfolios that dependably earn the highest return for the risk taken. This engineering process is about building balance, which involves an understanding of risk that requires looking under the hood of an asset, at what drives its returns.

Over some periods of time, bonds perform near the top of their range of expectations (represented by the cone) while stocks perform at the bottom of this range. Over other periods, the reverse is true. Understanding why this happens, allows us to combine complimentary assets in the right proportions so that during times when one underperforms the other will tend to outperform proportionally within the portfolio.

Same Return, with Less Risk

The results of balance speak for themselves. The asset allocations we manage for our clients can be expected to earn equity-like returns with only the risk level of government bonds and incur fewer and less frequent losses than traditional asset allocations.

Or, Higher Returns with Same Risk

The goal of passive investing, combined with a balanced asset allocation, is most efficiently earning a return on your investment. We help you do this while assuming the least amount of risk needed to meet your goals. Clients who experience the increased efficiency from a balanced portfolio can then choose how they want to use it. They can choose to take less risk, or boost return without increasing risk. We help you do this through our collaborative financial planning process.

All of our partners’ personal assets are invested using the same process along with those of their extended families ...we eat our own cooking.
