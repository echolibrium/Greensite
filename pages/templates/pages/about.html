Our People

Maneesh Shanbhag, CFA

Co-Founder

Maneesh is co-founder of Greenline Partners and is responsible for portfolio management, and custom portfolio engineering. Maneesh has spent the majority of his career managing portfolios for institutional investors, most recently at Bridgewater Associates where he and Peter first met. Maneesh was part of the portfolio management team at Bridgewater Associates that managed over $100 billion on behalf of some of the most sophisticated investors in the world, including large sovereign wealth funds, pensions, endowments and foundations. At Bridgewater, Maneesh also helped build a systematized investment process to manage customized accounts across equities, fixed income, currencies and commodities. He also advised investors in the United Kingdom, Europe and Canada on asset allocation and investment process design. Prior to Bridgewater, Maneesh managed tax efficient portfolios for family offices and high net worth individuals at a $2bn investment advisor in Illinois.

Maneesh has an MBA and MS in electrical engineering specializing in semiconductor physics from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, is a CFA Charterholder, and has been racing triathlons for over 20 years.

Peter Joers, III

Co-Founder

Peter is co-founder of Greenline Partners and serves on the investment committee. Prior to co-founding Greenline Partners, was a Senior Client Advisor at Bridgewater Associates, where he worked with some of the largest public pension plans, endowments and sovereign wealth funds on improving the risk adjusted returns of their asset allocation and active manager portfolios. Prior to joining Bridgewater, Peter was the President of Bank of America, Washington, a more than 6000 employee organization. He began his career with the firm 12 years earlier as a Managing Director with Montgomery Securities in San Francisco. During his career at Bank of America he managed a wealth management practice for US Trust working closely with some of the nation’s wealthiest families advising them on tax efficient portfolio management and risk management, trust and estate planning, engineering offsetting exposures to private assets and liabilities, private asset acquisition and financing, and advancing family office best practices.

Peter has served on numerous boards including the University of Washington’s Foster School of Business, Greater Seattle YMCA, Washington Round table and the Seattle Art Museum. Peter has an MBA from Southern Methodist University and a BA in Political Science from the University of Washington.

John Snyder, CPA

Partner

John Snyder has 36 years of investment experience working with individuals, families, pension funds and endowments. John spent 10 years at US Trust working with family offices on portfolio construction, risk management and estate planning . Prior to his role at US Trust, John was head of equities with Kennedy Associates, managing equity portfolios for pension funds and nonprofit organizations. John is a graduate of the University of Texas with a BBA in finance, and the University of Houston with a MS in Accountancy, after which he started his career as a CPA specializing in taxation for a former “Big Eight” firm.

John is active in the Seattle community and currently serves as the Vice Chair of Rainier Scholars, a local organization offering a pathway to college graduation for motivated, low income, children of color. Since its inception in 2002, Rainier Scholars has tutored and counseled over 500 children, beginning in 5th grade, with 150 now attending highly selective colleges and universities.
